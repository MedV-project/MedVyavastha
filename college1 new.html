How It Works

Designed for Emergencies

Simple steps for patients and hospitals to connect in critical moments.

01

Share Your Location

Allow MedVyavastha to detect your location or enter your city/area for nearby hospital suggestions.

02

See Live Availability

View real-time bed count, ICU capacity, ventilators, OT status, ambulances and emergency availability.

03

Contact & Navigate

Call the hospital directly, get directions, and confirm bed reservation before arriving.

04

Arrive Prepared

Hospital is already notified. Staff is ready for the patient on arrival — saving precious minutes.

For Hospitals

Powerful Inventory Dashboard

A single place to manage and publish your live capacity so patients and doctors always have accurate data.

Real-time Bed Management

Update general, ICU, and emergency beds instantly. Patients see live counts.

Equipment Inventory

Track ventilators, oxygen cylinders, dialysis machines and more in one dashboard.

OT Scheduling

Publish operation theatre availability and block slots for incoming emergencies.

Ambulance Fleet Tracking

Show ambulance availability and dispatch status to patients in real time.
