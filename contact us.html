Frequently Asked
Common Questions
Quick answers before you reach out.
How do I register my hospital on MedVyavastha?
Click "Register Hospital" in the top navigation, fill in your hospital's details across the 4-step form, and submit the required documents. Our team will verify and activate your listing within 24–48 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email with your registration ID.
The bed count / resource data for my hospital is wrong. How do I fix it?
Log in to your hospital dashboard and update the availability data in real time. If you don't have dashboard access, contact us using the form above with subject "Update Hospital Information" and we'll assist within a few hours.
Is MedVyavastha free for patients and families?
Yes — MedVyavastha is completely free for patients, families, and anyone searching for hospital information. We charge a nominal fee to hospitals for premium listing features and dashboard access.
How quickly does data on bed availability update?
Registered hospitals can update their availability data in real time through our dashboard. Data shown on the platform is as recent as the hospital's last update. We encourage hospitals to update at least every 4 hours during peak periods.
How long will it take to get a response to my message?
We aim to respond to all messages within 24 hours on business days. For urgent issues marked high priority, we try to respond within 4–6 hours. For immediate technical emergencies affecting hospital listings, call our helpline directly.
